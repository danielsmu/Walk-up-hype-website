Your privacy is real here.

End-to-end encryption.
What that means: we can't read what you type. Your submission is encrypted on your device before it ever leaves, and only your coach can unlock it.

No accounts. No personal data collected.
What that means: we don't know who you are. No login, no email, no phone number. Just a name and a song.

Auto-delete.
What that means: everything is wiped from our servers after 30 days. Your coach saves what they need to their phone, then the server copy is gone.

Anonymous analytics only.
What that means: we track basic, anonymous usage like "how many people completed the form" so we can make it better. We never see names, songs, or anything you type. No ads. No data sales. Ever.
