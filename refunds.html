Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Walk-Up Hype subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We are unable to issue refunds directly.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for a Walk-Up Hype purchase, visit Apple's refund page at reportaproblem.apple.com and follow the instructions. Apple handles all refund decisions for App Store purchases.

Free Trial

Walk-Up Hype Premium includes a 7-day free trial for new subscribers. You will not be charged if you cancel before the trial period ends. You can cancel at any time through your Apple ID subscription settings.

Questions

If you have questions about your purchase or need help, contact us at support@walkuphype.com and we'll do our best to help.
