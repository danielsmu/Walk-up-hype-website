Free Voice Upgrade

Unlock Minor League Mike

Minor League Mike is a free, genuinely good announcer voice — a big step up from the default. It takes about 2 minutes to set up and requires a one-time download in your iPhone's Accessibility settings.

💡

Why is it in Accessibility settings? Minor League Mike uses a high-quality enhanced voice that Apple ships as an optional download — the same technology used for screen readers. It's completely free, it just doesn't come pre-installed.

1

Open the Settings app on your iPhone

This is the gray gear icon on your home screen.

2

Go to Accessibility

Scroll down and tap Accessibility.

Settings → Accessibility
3

Tap Spoken Content

Under the Vision section, tap Spoken Content.

Accessibility → Spoken Content
4

Tap Voices

At the top of the Spoken Content screen, tap Voices.

5

Select English

Tap English from the language list.

6

Find Evan and download the Enhanced version

Scroll through the voice list to find Evan. Tap it to expand, then tap the download icon next to Evan (Enhanced). The download is about 200MB — connect to Wi-Fi first if you're on cellular.

7

Wait for the download to finish

A progress indicator will appear next to Evan (Enhanced). Once it disappears and a checkmark shows, the voice is ready.

8

Open Walk-Up Hype and select Minor League Mike

Go to your team's settings in Walk-Up Hype, tap Announcer Voice, and select Minor League Mike. He'll now be available as your free voice.

🎙️

Ready to go.

Minor League Mike sounds like a real announcer — not a robot. Your kids will love it. When you're ready for the full stadium experience, Premium unlocks 5 more voices including Big League Bill, Coach Carlos, and Dugout Donna.
