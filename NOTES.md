# Walk-Up Hype — Notes & To-Dos

## App Store Description
The copy from the "Is free mode actually free?" FAQ answer is strong and should
be considered for the App Store description. Specifically this passage:

> "We built a real working app for free users because not every family can
> justify another subscription — and every kid deserves a walk-up moment,
> regardless... We've worked hard to make Premium worth your business, but if
> that's not in the budget right now, we hope the free mode gives your kid
> their moment. It's yours, free of charge, for as long as Walk-Up Hype is on
> the App Store."

The tone is sincere, human, and differentiates the app from competitors who
use free mode purely as a conversion funnel. Worth weaving into the App Store
description before the next major update or App Store editorial submission.
