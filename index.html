FAQ

Do I need Apple Music?

For walk-up songs, yes. Music streams directly from Apple Music, so you'll need an active subscription. No Apple Music? Announcements still fire — your kids still get their moment.

What does the free announcer voice sound like?

Way better than you'd expect. The free tier uses a real AI neural voice — not the robotic iPhone voice you're thinking of. Premium unlocks 5 custom PA announcer voices with more character and energy. But free sounds good. We made sure of it.

Does it work offline or at fields with bad signal?

Mostly yes. Announcements that have already played are cached on your device — no signal needed. Generating a brand new one requires a connection. Apple Music needs signal to stream. Load up before you head to the field and you're covered.

How many teams and players can I manage?

Free is 1 team, up to 10 players — plenty for a single squad. Premium is unlimited. Coach two teams? Run a whole league? Go for it.

What happens when the free trial ends?

You drop to the free tier automatically. Your data stays intact — all your teams, players, and announcements are still there. You just can't add new teams beyond 1 or new players beyond 10 until you resubscribe.
