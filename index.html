FAQ

Do I need Apple Music?

For walk-up songs, yes. Music streams directly from Apple Music, so you'll need an active subscription. No Apple Music? Announcements still fire — your kids still get their moment.

What do I get with Premium?

Five stadium-quality PA announcer voices — Big League Bill, Play by Play Pete, Ringside Randy, Dugout Donna, and Coach Carlos (bilingual English/Spanish). Unlimited teams and players. A 7-day free trial, then $9.99/year. That's it. No hidden tiers, no per-feature unlocks. If you're running multiple teams or you want your kids to sound like they're walking up at a real ballpark, Premium is worth every penny.

Is free mode actually free, or just a demo?

Actually free. No timer, no watermark, no "upgrade to continue." We built a real working app for free users because not every family can justify another subscription — and every kid deserves a walk-up moment, regardless. One team, up to 10 players (that's nine on the field plus one extra), walk-up music, between-innings playlist, Game Mode — the whole experience. It's not a taste. It's the thing. We've worked hard to make Premium worth your business, but if that's not in the budget right now, we hope the free mode gives your kid their moment. It's yours, free of charge, for as long as Walk-Up Hype is on the App Store.

What does the free announcer voice sound like?

Honest answer: the default free voice is rough. It works, but it sounds like a robot reading a spreadsheet. Here's the thing though — there's a second free voice called Minor League Mike that's a real step up. It's still free, just requires a one-time download in your iPhone's Accessibility settings. We walk you through it in the app. If you want to actually impress parents on day one, Minor League Mike is the move. Premium is still the big leagues — 5 custom PA voices that sound like an actual stadium — but free isn't a dead end.

Does it work offline or at fields with bad signal?

Mostly yes. Announcements that have already played are cached on your device — no signal needed. For walk-up music, if you've downloaded your songs in the Apple Music app they'll play offline too. Streaming requires signal. Load up before you head to the field and you're covered.

How many teams and players can I manage?

Free is 1 team, up to 10 players — plenty for a single squad. Premium is unlimited. Coach two teams? Run a whole league? Go for it.

What happens when the free trial ends?

You drop to the free tier automatically. All your data stays intact — your teams, players, and announcements are still there. You just can't add a second team or add players beyond 10 until you resubscribe. Resubscribe and everything unlocks immediately.
